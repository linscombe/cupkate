About our Cakes

The LBS family has been making birthday, wedding and any-event-you-can-think of cakes for the past 17 years! And we will continue to for your family. We can make anything from an elegant 6 tiered cake for newlyweds to a Mickey Mouse themed smash cake. Our cakes can be ordered in 10 different, classic flavours. Custom orders are also accepted for any cake! Get an original flavour, topping, color or shape.

Any amount of tiers, sizes are . Set price is $3/slice for birthday cakes and $7/slice for wedding cakes. Extra customizations (fondant details, piping details, merangue, etc..) start at $4/tier that is customized. We require a 2 week notice before the event takes place for BIRTHDAY CAKE customizations, and a 6 week notice for WEDDING CAKE customizations so that we have time to plan, buy, and create!
