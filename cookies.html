About our Cookies

Even simple and typical desserts, like cookies, can be very compelling. The best cookies have layers of texture. A slightly crisp outer shell that holds up to some heat with an inner core that's soft and chewy. We pride ourselves in using all natural ingredients and flavours that will make your body and tastebuds happy! You can click here to look at some brands we use.

Set price is $21.99 for any flavours, with no customizations in a dozen. Extra customizations (Icing details, stamps, shapes, etc..) start at $3.50/ cookie that is customized. We require a 2 week notice before the event takes place for cookie customizations, so we have time to buy ingredients and/or supplies!
