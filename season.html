About our Seasonal Flavours

Seasonal Flavours are unique flavours that we make available for our customers depending on the season! WHen they are in season, these flavours will be the same price as our standard flavours. Anytime out of their season, they will cost more than the usual price, so make sure you fill up! Check the chart below for the current season's flavours and subscribe to our newsletter for a sneak peek of each season's new selection and release date!

Any customizations (fondant details, piping details, stamps, figurines, custom messages , etc...) start at regular customization prices. Click here to view the prices for cupcakes without customizations, here for the prices of cakes without customizations, here for the prices of cookies without customizations. View your chosen dessert's page for details on prices for customization.
