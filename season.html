About our Seasonality Specials

"Seasonality Specials" or "Seasonal Flavours" are unique flavours that we make available for the community depending on the season! During their season, these flavours with be the same price as our default flavours. Anytime out of their season, they will cost more than the usual price, so make sure you snatch em' up. Check the chart below for the current season's flavours and subscribe to our newsletter for a sneak peek of each season's new selection and release date!

Click here to view the prices for cupcakes without customizations, here for the prices of cakes without customizations, here for the prices of cookies without customizations. Any customizations (fondant details, piping details, stamps, figurines, custom messages , etc..) start at regular customization prices. We require a 3 week notice for CELEBRATION CAKE customizations, a 7 week notice for WEDDING CAKE customizations, a 2 week notice for COOKIE customizations, and a 2 week notice for CUPCAKE customizations. THIS MUST BE THE AMOUNT OF WEEKS STATED BEFORE THE EVENT TAKES PLACE.
