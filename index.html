Why choose "La Bonne Sucrerie"

Our number one priority at LBS is making our costumers happy. We value high quality ingredients, and
our buyers satisfaction. Here are some of the reasons we should be your number one choice!

  • High Quality Control

    Our lead priority is keeping costumers happy and healthy. We wash our hands regularly, use gloves, fully sanitize our tools & workspace after every order and use food-safe packaging.

  • There's Always A Cake

    You will never be left cake-less. If you have an order, custom or not, we will always go out of our way to fullfill it in a timely manner.

  • 17+ Years of Experience

    We have over 17 years of experience, and previous training since 2004 in the art of creating your favorite desserts.

  • Earth Lovers

    La Bonne Sucrerie loves our planet and wants to keep it sustainable. That's why we use compostable, biodegradable and 100% recyclable packaging.

  • We are There for You

    We sell alternatives to some of our desserts and we can work with your dietary restrictions or allergies.

  • Fresh Ingredients

    We always use high quality ingredients and seasonal products when possible. Dome brands we use are: "King Arthur" and "Bob's Redmill" Flours, La Tourgenelle oils, organic diary brands.
